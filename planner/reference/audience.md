# PIQI Group Audience

PIQI is an octopus over six verticals. Each arm has its own audience — there is no single "PIQI customer". Plus one cross-vertical group visitor (journalists / partners / investors) who explores the umbrella.

---

## Segment 1 — Consulting (B2B decision-makers)

**Who:** Mid-to-large SA enterprises. Operations directors, project sponsors, COOs, programme managers in capital-heavy industries (mining, infrastructure, manufacturing, logistics).

**What they need:** Outside expertise on commercial controls, project FEL, supply chain interventions, cost recovery on troubled projects.

**How they find us:** Referrals primarily; secondarily LinkedIn / direct search for "project commercial services South Africa". Will research PIQI's credibility before contacting.

**What converts:** Proof of capability (case studies — currently a blocker), credentialed bios (also blocker), specific deliverables on the Consulting spokes, fast email reply.

---

## Segment 2 — Property Management (SA property owners + tenants)

**Who:** SA residential and small-commercial landlords; tenants seeking responsive management.

**What they need:** A landlord wants reliable maintenance, tenant screening, rent collection, and minimal personal involvement. A tenant wants fast emergency response and clear lease handling.

**How they find us:** Local search ("property management Gardenview / Johannesburg"), word of mouth, Property24-style listing crossover.

**What converts:** Local trust signals, fast response promise, clear scope of services, easy contact.

---

## Segment 3 — Yacht Charters (charter customers)

**Who:** Affluent local + international leisure travellers; small private groups; couples celebrating; corporate getaways.

**What they need:** A self-catering catamaran experience, freedom to set the itinerary, transparency on what's included (fuel for short shore trips) vs. not (food, drink, longer fuel).

**How they find us:** Travel blogs, charter aggregators, direct search for the home port (TBD), Instagram word of mouth.

**What converts:** Vessel photography, cabin layout, pricing transparency (all currently TBD blockers), reviews (none yet).

---

## Segment 4 — Auto / Piqi Auto (local SA retail)

**Who:** Local SA car owners needing panel beating after collision or scratch repair; tyre shoppers.

**What they need:** Honest assessment, clear pricing, quick turnaround. For tyres: convenient supply with a clear path to fitment (currently a copy/ops gap — Piqi sells but doesn't fit).

**How they find us:** Local search, insurance assessor referrals (panel beating), word of mouth.

**What converts:** Honesty about the fitment gap, before/after work examples, location clarity.

---

## Segment 5 — Fashion / Downtown Fashion (local SA retail)

**Who:** Brides and grooms-to-be; matric students; schools; corporates ordering uniforms; safety / branded apparel buyers.

**What they need:** Bespoke craftsmanship, on-time delivery for events (weddings, matric), bulk uniform reliability.

**How they find us:** Instagram, word of mouth, school/corporate procurement contacts.

**What converts:** Photographs of past garments (need to source — likely blocker), turnaround timelines, fittings process explanation.

---

## Segment 6 — Coaching (individuals & execs)

**Who:** Executives wanting leadership / communication coaching; professionals seeking work-life balance support.

**What they need:** A discreet, qualified coach. Confidentiality matters. Want to understand the coach's approach before committing.

**How they find us:** LinkedIn, referrals from HR teams, direct search.

**What converts:** Coach credentials (TBD blocker — no team named), clarity on confidentiality, low-friction first-conversation offer.

---

## Cross-Vertical — Group Visitor (journalists / partners / investors)

**Who:** Trade press writing about SA group conglomerates; potential partners exploring co-investment; family-office or PE researchers; suppliers wanting to pitch the group.

**What they need:** Quick orientation to the group's scope, ownership clarity (TBD), single contact route.

**How they find us:** Search for "PIQI Group", LinkedIn, news mentions.

**What converts:** Clean About page in group voice, easy Contact, clear list of what the group does and does not do (no overclaim of synergy across arms).

**They land on:** Homepage → About → Contact. They do not need to dive into individual vertical hubs unless one is relevant to their inquiry.

---

## Audiences to Avoid

- **Single-arm price-shoppers** trying to play verticals against each other (PIQI's verticals are not interchangeable).
- **Anyone seeking Banking services** — deferred. Make this clear if asked.
- **Cross-border consulting RFPs outside southern Africa** unless explicitly scoped — keep focus on SA market.
