# PIQI Group — The Six-Arm Octopus

PIQI Group operates six verticals as siblings under a thin umbrella. Only Consulting has sub-services in v1.

---

## 1. Consulting (`/consulting`)

**Positioning:** PIQI's advisory practice — protecting commercial outcomes on capital projects and operational change programmes.

**Primary customer:** B2B decision-makers — operations directors, programme managers, project sponsors in mid-to-large SA enterprises.

**Maturity:** Live on existing site; the most developed vertical in terms of marketed copy.

### Sub-services (5 spokes in v1)

#### 1a. Business Process — `/consulting/business-process`
**Positioning:** Analyse, identify, streamline. Improve operational performance and tighten how the business runs day-to-day.
**Sourced from live site:** "Process Consulting" + "Operational Performance".
**Deliverables:** process audits, bottleneck analysis, operating-model redesign, performance baselines.

#### 1b. Strategy — `/consulting/strategy`
**Positioning:** Strategic business planning that moves from boardroom statement to executable plan.
**Sourced from live site:** "Business Strategy" + "Strategic Business Planning".
**Deliverables:** strategy capture workshops, planning artefacts, growth/profitability/competitive-position frameworks.

#### 1c. Project Management — `/consulting/project-management`
**Positioning:** On-time, on-budget delivery — planning, risk, resources, stakeholder communication, lifecycle support.
**Sourced from live site:** "Project Management" + "Product/Service Lifecycle".
**Deliverables:** project planning, risk registers, resource allocation, stakeholder cadences, lifecycle reviews.

#### 1d. Supply Chain — `/consulting/supply-chain`
**Positioning:** Operational control across procurement-through-delivery.
**Sourced from live site:** "Supply Chain Support".
**Deliverables:** sourcing strategy, supplier performance, logistics interventions, cost-out programmes.

#### 1e. Commercial Services — `/consulting/commercial-services`
**Positioning:** Cradle-to-grave project commercial support — protecting outcomes from FEL through close-out.
**Sourced from live site:** "Project Commercial Services" + "FEL: Set-up for success".
**Deliverables:**
- Procurement
- Contracts Management
- Claims Management
- Negotiation
- Cost Control
- Planning Development
- Risk Management
- FEL ("Set-up for success") project reviews

## 2. Property Management (`/property`)

**Positioning:** End-to-end property operations — maintenance, letting, construction support — handled.

**Primary customer:** SA property owners (residential + small commercial); tenants of managed properties.

**Maturity:** Live on existing site.

**Core offerings:** routine inspections, preventive maintenance, emergency response, tenant screening, lease management, rent collection, property marketing, renovations, remodeling, new construction.

---

## 3. Fashion — Downtown Fashion (`/fashion`)

**Positioning:** Bespoke clothing, made for the day it's worn.

**Primary customer:** Local SA retail — bridal parties, matric dance attendees, schools, corporate uniform buyers, safety/branded apparel buyers.

**Maturity:** Live on existing site under "Downtown Fashion" sub-brand.

**Core offerings:** wedding attire (bridal, bridesmaids, flower girls, groom, best man); matric dance outfits; bespoke high-fashion; school uniforms; tracksuits; safety clothing; embroidery and printing on branded clothing.

---

## 4. Yacht Charters (`/yachts`)

**Positioning:** A self-catering catamaran charter — your itinerary, your provisions.

**Primary customer:** Charter customers — small private groups, couples, corporate getaways.

**Maturity:** Live on existing site at concept level. **Vessel name, home port, and pricing are blockers** for full hub copy.

**Core offering:** Catamaran with 4 double cabins, self-catering, fuel for short shore trips included. Guests bring their own food and drink.

---

## 5. Auto — Piqi Auto (`/auto`)

**Positioning:** Honest panel beating and tyre supply.

**Primary customer:** Local SA retail — collision-repair customers and tyre buyers.

**Maturity:** Live on existing site.

**Core offerings:**
- Panel beating: dents, scratches, major repairs.
- Tyre supply: range of tyres in stock.
- **Gap:** does NOT fit tyres. Copy must either recommend an external fitment partner or be reworked once a partnership exists. Logged as a blocker.

## 6. Coaching (`/coaching`)

**Positioning:** Practical coaching for leaders and individuals — focused, confidential, outcome-led.

**Primary customer:** Individuals and executives.

**Maturity:** Live on existing site at concept level.

**Core offerings:** leadership enhancement; communication skills; work-life balance.
