# PIQI Group — Brand Tone of Voice

PIQI uses a **Virgin-style dual register**: a corporate-neutral umbrella voice on the group chrome, and a distinct voice per vertical sub-brand. Never mix the two.

---

## Group Voice (umbrella — Home, About, Contact)

**Personality:** corporate-neutral, confident, factual, restrained.

**Sounds like:** "PIQI Group operates an advisory practice and a portfolio of asset-based businesses across South Africa. Each arm runs on its own terms."

**Avoid:** breathless synergy claims, "we do everything" hand-waving, jargon, marketing fluff that overclaims connection between verticals.

---

## Per-Vertical Tone Matrix

Each vertical owns its voice. Copy on a vertical hub should read as if the sub-brand wrote it — not as if PIQI Group wrote it about the vertical.

| Vertical | Tone adjectives | Sounds like | Avoid |
|----------|-----------------|-------------|-------|
| **Consulting** | precise / credentialed / outcome-focused | "We protect commercial outcomes through procurement, contracts, claims, and cost control." | hyperbole, vague promises, generic "thought leadership" |
| **Property Management** | reliable / local / responsive | "Tenant screening, lease management, fast emergency response — the day-to-day handled." | corporate distance, real-estate-broker glamour |
| **Fashion (Downtown Fashion)** | celebratory / bespoke / warm | "Wedding suits, matric outfits, school uniforms — made for the day they're worn." | luxury-brand snobbery, jargon |
| **Yacht Charters** | aspirational / experiential | "A self-catering catamaran. Four double cabins. Your itinerary." | hard sell, business-speak |
| **Auto (Piqi Auto)** | practical / transparent | "Panel beating done properly. Tyres in stock — and we'll point you to who fits them." | over-promise, hidden caveats |
| **Coaching** | encouraging / direct / confidential | "Leadership, communication, work-life balance — practical sessions, full confidentiality." | guru-speak, mystical tone |

## Communication Rules

### General
- **Greeting style:** Group voice = neutral / "Get in touch". Vertical voices = each defines their own.
- **Formatting:** Working-doc tight. Short paragraphs. Bullet lists when scanning is the goal.
- **Honesty over polish:** Where a service has a gap (e.g., Piqi Auto doesn't fit tyres), say so on the page rather than hide it.

### Website Copy
- **Do:** active voice, second person, outcome-led, specific deliverables.
- **Don't:** unexplained jargon, walls of text, "leverage", "synergize", "best-in-class" filler.

### Service Descriptions (Consulting in particular)
- **Do:** lead with the problem, name the deliverables, use numbers when available.
- **Don't:** vague "we partner with you on your journey" pap.

### Contact / Email
- **Do:** acknowledge within 48 hours. Reply in the voice of the vertical the inquiry came from.
- **Don't:** form letters across all verticals.

### Cross-vertical posture
- **Do:** acknowledge the group exists when relevant, link only via top nav.
- **Don't:** push someone reading about Yachts to also read about Auto.
